To maximize reach, stars, and engagement for **GitPulse**, launch the project with highly visual, structured social media posts on LinkedIn and Twitter/X.

## 📸 Media Strategy (Crucial)
1. **Record a 15-second Screen Capture**: Record yourself typing in your username, hitting **TRACE PULSE**, watching the loader progress, and viewing the slides (especially slide 1 avatar zoom-in and slide 3 language charts drawing).
2. **Convert to a high-quality GIF or MP4**: Attach this video to your posts. Visuals drive 10x more reach than text-only posts, especially for front-end developers.
